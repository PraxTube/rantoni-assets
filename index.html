Martial Artist character for your 2D pixel art game. Works well in 2D top-down as the animations are 4-directional. May also work in a 2D side-scroller.

FREE Version

Contains the following animations:

  • Idle
  • Run
  • Stagger (+recover)
  • Jab (+recover)
  • Punch (+recover)
  • Front Kick (+recover)
  • Death
